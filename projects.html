GoRides

GoRides is a comprehensive ride-hailing solution designed to offer seamless transportation services through two dedicated mobile applications. The Passenger app allows users to easily book rides by selecting their preferred car type and inputting their destination. Upon requesting a ride, the app automatically sends the request to nearby drivers, minimizing wait times. With integrated wallet functionality, passengers can make secure and effortless payments through the Flutterwave payment gateway. Additionally, the app features a referral code system, enabling users to earn rewards by inviting new users, which promotes engagement and drives platform growth.

Technologies Used:

  • Firebase: Used for real-time database and user authentication.
  • Flutterwave: Integrated payment gateway for secure transactions.
  • Google Maps API: Provides real-time location tracking and route optimization.
